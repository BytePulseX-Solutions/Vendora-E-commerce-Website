Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Privacy Overview

Introduction

Vendora is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection.

Data We Gather

Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email address, and phone number
  • Account credentials and profile information
  • Order, payment, and transaction details
  • Device, browser, and IP address information
Collection Process

How We Collect Information

We collect information when you register an account, place an order, fill out forms, contact support, or interact with our website using cookies and analytics tools.

Usage & Purpose

How We Use Your Information

  • To process orders and manage transactions
  • To provide customer support and communication
  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • To send updates, promotions, and service notifications
Tracking & Privacy

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance site functionality, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Privacy & Security

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared with trusted third-party partners such as payment processors, logistics providers, and analytics services strictly for operational purposes.

Privacy & Protection

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure servers, and restricted access to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or misuse.

Your Privacy Controls

User Rights & Choices

  • Access and review your personal data
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
Retaining Your Information

Data Retention Policy

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill legal, operational, and contractual obligations.

Stay Informed

Contact & Policy Updates

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team. We may update this policy periodically, and all changes will be reflected here to ensure transparency.
