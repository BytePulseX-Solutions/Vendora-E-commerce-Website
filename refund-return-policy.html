Refund & Return Policy

Policy Overview

At Vendora, customer satisfaction is our priority. This Refund and Return Policy explains the conditions under which you may return products purchased from our store and request refunds or exchanges.

Returns Policy

Eligibility for Returns

  • Items must be returned within 14 days of delivery
  • Products must be unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging and tags must be intact
Important Notice

Non-Returnable Items

  • Digital or downloadable products
  • Gift cards or vouchers
  • Clearance or final sale items
  • Customized or personalized products
How to Return Products

Return Process

  1. Contact our support team to initiate a return
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Ship the item using the provided return instructions
  4. Wait for confirmation after inspection
Important Information

Refund Policy

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Important Information

Exchanges & Shipping Costs

  • Exchanges are subject to product availability
  • Return shipping costs are customer’s responsibility
  • Damaged or incorrect items qualify for free return shipping
Customer Assistance

Need Help?

If you have any questions regarding refunds or returns, our support team is here to help.
